### Reviews Summary

Bodega de la Ardosa is a beloved, historic tapas bar offering an authentic Madrid experience. Patrons rave about the legendary tortilla and lively, time-traveling ambiance. While some find the prices a bit high for the portion sizes and service can be a bit hurried due to its popularity, the unique atmosphere and delicious food make it a must-visit.

"Bodega de Ardosa offers excellent food in a very original, atmospheric setting that feels full of character. The dishes were flavorful and clearly made with care—great for those looking for an authentic local experience. While the service was generally good, it did feel a bit tired or overworked, which slightly took away from the overall vibe. Still, it’s absolutely worth a visit for the food and ambiance."

Frequently Asked Questions
--------------------------

What is Bodega de la Ardosa famous for?

It's most famous for its award-winning tortilla de patatas (Spanish omelet), as well as its authentic, historic ambiance and traditional tapas.

![Image 67: TikTok](https://www.google.com/s2/favicons?sz=128&domain=tiktok.com)![Image 68: Restaurants in Madrid](https://itin-dev.wanderlogstatic.com/webPlacesListSite/65)![Image 69: Madrid’s oldest restaurants and bars offer tapas, vino and history](https://itin-dev.wanderlogstatic.com/webPlacesListSite/113)+1

Is it easy to get a seat at Bodega de la Ardosa?

No, it is usually very busy. Many patrons stand at barrels, and there's a small back area accessible by ducking under the bar.

![Image 70: TikTok](https://www.google.com/s2/favicons?sz=128&domain=tiktok.com)![Image 71: Where To Eat When You’re Visiting Madrid - Madrid](https://itin-dev.wanderlogstatic.com/webPlacesListSite/47)![Image 72: The Most Historic Bars In Madrid](https://itin-dev.wanderlogstatic.com/webPlacesListSite/22)+2

What are the must-try tapas?
