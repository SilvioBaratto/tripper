### Overview

**Vibrant rooftop bar and restaurant, perched on top of the Círculo de Bellas Artes.**

Azotea del Circulo (which translates into the Circle Roof) is one of [Madrid's best rooftop bars](https://www.therooftopguide.com/rooftop-bars-in-madrid.html), with a central location, fantastic views and generous opening hours.

From the spacious roof terrace you get an absolutely stunning 360-view of Madrid's skyline. Perhaps the best view in the whole city.

Up here you find a rooftop restaurant that delivers great Spanish and Mediterranean food throughout the day. From breakfast, tapas and lunch, to dinner and weekend brunch.

The rooftop bar serves all the classic drinks, with plenty of wine and beer, sangria, champagne and refreshing cocktails, all offered at very reasonable prices.

Equipped with plenty of comfortable and stylish loungers and classic bar stools, Azotea del Circulo also holds more dining-suitable tables, and a glass-house hut with retractable roof and walls, making it a year-round venue.

Vibrant and popular, this is a great place for anything from a long and sunny rooftop lunch, to sunset cocktails and fun nights.
