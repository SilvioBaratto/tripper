### Audio guide

#### Enjoy an audio-guided tour of the collection and temporary exhibitions

*   Ticket + €5
*   More than 250 works from the collection explained in 15 languages
*   The main temporary exhibitions in Spanish and English
*   Available on Museum devices or on your smartphone
*   Free loan of sign language guides, audio guides with audio description and magnetic loop systems

![Image 9: A guide explains a work to a group of visitors](https://content3.cdnprado.net/imagenes/proyectos/personalizacion/7317a29a-d846-4c54-9034-6a114c3658fe/cms/visita_guiadas_th2.jpg)

### Guided Tours

#### Visit the Museum with us

*   Duration: about 90 minutes
*   Ticket + 10€

#### Permanent Collection Masterpieces

*   Español: 10.30 am, 12.30 pm, 3.15 pm and 5.00 pm
*   English: 11.00 am and 1.00 pm
