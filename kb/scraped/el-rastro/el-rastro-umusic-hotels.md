EL RASTRO MARKET IN MADRID
==========================

![Image 8: portrait image: EL RASTRO MARKET IN MADRID](https://images2.neobookings.com/cms/umusic-hotels.com/section/el-rastro-market-in-madrid/pics/el-rastro-market-in-madrid-v5glqz9l0q.jpeg?width=1110)

Hola, fellow travelers! If you're planning a trip to the vibrant city of Madrid, there's one place that's an absolute must-visit:**El Rastro Market**. This isn't just any old market; it's the most popular**open-air flea market**in Madrid, with a history that dates back over 400 years!

If you don’t know[what to do in Madrid](https://umusic-hotels.com/umusic-hotel-madrid/happenings/what-to-do-in-madrid/), one of the best plans is without doubt visiting this famous market. Every Sunday and on public holidays, El Rastro Madrid comes alive with locals and tourists alike. It's not just about shopping; it's an experience that truly embodies the spirit of this amazing city. So get ready to delve into the bustling lanes of El Rastro Market and discover its many treasures!

**EL RASTRO MADRID LOCATION**

The legendary El Rastro Market is nestled**right in the heart of the city**, located along and around Calle de la Ribera de Curtidores, between Ronda de Toledo to the north and Ronda de Valencia to the south. The vibrant neighborhood of**La Latina**hosts this bustling market every Sunday. It's a stone's throw away from the La Latina metro station, making it super accessible for both locals and tourists alike. So whether you're staying uptown, downtown or anywhere around town, finding El Rastro won't be a puzzle!

**WHEN IS EL RASTRO MADRID OPEN?**

Ready to experience the hustle and bustle of El Rastro Madrid? Well, you better set your alarm early! This lively market only**opens on Sundays and public holidays**. But don't worry, it's not a crack-of-dawn kind of thing. The market starts buzzing**from 9:00 am**and keeps going**until 3:00 pm**. So, whether you're an early bird or more of a late riser, there's plenty of time for you to explore what El Rastro has to offer. Just remember, the earlier you get there, the better the treasures you'll find!

**WHAT CAN YOU FIND AT EL RASTRO MADRID?**

El Rastro flea market is a treasure trove of goods and products that cater to every whim and fancy. It's the kind of place where you can find just about anything, from**vintage clothes**and**antique furniture**to**rare vinyl records**and unique**art pieces**.

As you stroll through the bustling lanes of El Rastro Madrid, you'll come across a**wide variety of stalls**selling everything from quirky trinkets to high-quality leather goods. Whether you're on the hunt for a one-of-a-kind souvenir or simply browsing for the fun of it, there's always something interesting to discover at this vibrant flea market. And let's not forget about the food! The area surrounding the market is full of**tapas bars and restaurants**where you can indulge in some delicious Spanish cuisine after a long day of shopping. So, if you're planning on visiting Madrid, make sure El Rastro is on your list!

**THE BEST ACCOMMODATION TO VISIT MADRID’S EL RASTRO FLEA MARKET**

After a day of haggling and exploring at El Rastro, what could be better than retiring to the luxurious comfort of the**UMusic Hotel Madrid?**Nestled in the heart of the city, this[**5-star hotel in Madrid**](https://umusic-hotels.com/umusic-hotel-madrid/)is more than just a place to rest your head. It's an experience in itself!

With its unique**combination of accommodation and live shows**, you'll get to enjoy the vibrant music scene that Madrid is famous for without even having to step outside. The rooms are top-notch, with all the amenities you'd expect from a 5-star establishment. Plus, its**central location**means easy access not just to El Rastro but also other must-visit spots in Madrid.

**HOW TO GET TO EL RASTRO FROM UMUSIC HOTEL MADRID**

The UMusic Hotel is right in the center of Madrid, which makes getting to El Rastro Market a breeze. From our hotel, the market is a short**8-minute walk**, heading south down Calle de Toledo. You can also grab a taxi for 5 minutes. Don't forget to take in the sights along the way; it's part of the fun of visiting El Rastro Madrid!

If you get hungry after walking through El Rastro, check out our[tour of the best tapas in Madrid](https://umusic-hotels.com/umusic-hotel-madrid/happenings/tour-tapas-madrid/)and enjoy some Spanish tapas paired with a beer or wine.
